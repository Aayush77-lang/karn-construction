Nepal's Trusted Construction & Earthmoving Partner

Karn Construction Pvt Ltd has been at the forefront of Nepal's infrastructure development for over 15 years. We specialize in construction and earthmoving services that transform landscapes and build the foundation for progress.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery has earned us the trust of government bodies, private enterprises, and communities across Nepal. With a fleet of 7 modern excavators and JCB machines, we have the capacity to handle projects of any scale.

Our Services

Road Construction

Building Construction
